Learn More About Environmental Conservation

Tips for Recycling

Learn how to properly sort your recyclables and reduce waste:

  • Recycle paper, plastic, glass, and metal.
  • Reduce single-use plastics by opting for reusable items.
  • Buy products made from recycled materials.

Saving Energy

Simple practices to conserve energy and reduce your carbon footprint:

  • Turn off lights when not in use.
  • Use energy-efficient appliances.
  • Unplug devices when not in use to save energy.

Eco-Friendly Tips for Everyday Life

Every small action can make a big difference in protecting the planet:

  • Use public transport, carpool, or bike instead of driving alone.
  • Opt for sustainable fashion brands.
  • Plant trees and support green spaces in your community.
