About the Environmental Conservation Game

Why This Game?

The Environmental Conservation Game is designed to raise awareness about recycling, composting, and reducing waste. Through fun and interactive gameplay, users can learn how to make better choices for the planet.

How to Play

In this game, you will be asked to drag and drop items into the correct recycling, composting, or trash bins. Each correctly placed item earns you points, while incorrect choices will reset after a short delay.

Our Mission

Our goal is to make environmental conservation fun and accessible for everyone. We believe small actions lead to big changes, and through education and gameplay, we hope to inspire people to make sustainable choices every day.
